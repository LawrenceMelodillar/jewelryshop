Diamond

Known as the "king of gemstones," diamonds are prized for their hardness and brilliance. They come in a variety of colors, with colorless diamonds being the most valuable.

Price:$1,000 - $10,000+ per carat (depending on quality)

Ruby

A precious red gemstone, rubies are considered one of the four "precious" stones alongside diamonds, emeralds, and sapphires. The most prized rubies are deep red, often referred to as "pigeon blood" rubies.

Price:$200 - $5,000+ per carat (higher prices for high-quality stones).

Emerald

A rich green gemstone, emeralds are prized for their vibrant color. They belong to the beryl family and often have natural inclusions called "jardin," which can affect their clarity.

Price:$200 - $5,000+ per carat (higher prices for high-quality stones).

Sapphire

Typically blue, sapphires can also be found in a variety of colors. They are one of the hardest gemstones and are often used in fine jewelry.

Price:$20 to $400 per carat. (higher prices for high-quality stones).

Amethyst

Amethyst is a purple variety of quartz and is known for its rich color. It has been a popular gemstone for centuries and is often used in rings, earrings, and necklaces.

Price:$10 to $1,500+ per carat. (higher prices for high-quality stones).

Opal

>Opals are known for their vibrant play-of-color, where different colors flash across the surface of the stone. They come in various colors, but the most prized opals show a range of hues in one stone.

Price:$10 to $300 per carat.(higher prices for high-quality stones).

Topaz

Topaz is available in a variety of colors, from colorless to yellow, blue, and pink. The blue variety is particularly popular in jewelry.

Price:$20 to $400 per carat. (higher prices for high-quality stones).

Garnet

Garnet is a striking gemstone known for its deep red color, though it can also come in green, orange, and yellow. It is often seen as a symbol of energy, strength, and passion. Garnet is believed to promote health and vitality, making it a popular choice for both jewelry and healing purposes.

: $120 per carat

Peridot

Peridot is a beautiful green gemstone known for its vibrant color and clarity. It is often associated with positive energy, abundance, and renewal. Peridot is believed to bring good luck and happiness, making it a popular choice for jewelry.

Price:$150 per carat
